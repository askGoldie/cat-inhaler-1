üîÑ Puff Counter Logic

This is the most critical feature - accuracy is required at all times

Decrement Rules (subtract 1 puff):

  • Morning dose checked ‚Üí -1 puff
  • Evening dose checked ‚Üí -1 puff
  • Extra puff logged ‚Üí -1 puff

Increment Rules (add 1 puff back):

  • Morning dose unchecked ‚Üí +1 puff
  • Evening dose unchecked ‚Üí +1 puff
  • Extra puff deleted ‚Üí +1 puff

Examples:

Starting: 120 puffs ‚Üì Morning dose given 119 puffs ‚Üì Evening dose given 118 puffs ‚Üì Extra puff logged (Brad squirmed) 117 puffs ‚Üì Realize morning dose was actually not given, uncheck it 118 puffs ‚Üì Actually give morning dose, check it 117 puffs

üì± Mobile-First Design Principles

  • Touch targets: All interactive elements minimum 44x44px (checkboxes shown as 40x40px in wireframes)
  • Card layout: Distinct sections separated by cards for visual clarity
  • Single column: All content stacks vertically for mobile readability
  • Large typography: Important numbers (puff count) are prominently displayed
  • Color coding: Green = completed, Yellow/Orange = warning, White = pending
  • Minimal scrolling: Most important info (doses, puff count) above the fold
